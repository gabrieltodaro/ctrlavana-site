# Press Kit

### Ctrlavana for iPhone
**Operating System:** iOS 16.0 or later  
**Compatibility:** iPhone  
**Price:** Free

## App Description

**Ctrlavana** is the ultimate app for managing your excursions. Organize your caravans, keep track of passengers, and always have important information at hand—without needing an internet connection. **Ctrlavana** ensures everything is within reach in a practical and secure way.

### Key Features:

- Passenger management with detailed information (name, phone, email).
- Integration with messaging and calling services directly from the app.
- View caravan origin and destination.
- iCloud calendar sync.
- Works 100% offline, ensuring total data privacy.
