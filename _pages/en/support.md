# Support

## Getting Started

To begin using Ctrlavana:

1. Create a caravan
2. Add passengers
3. Set departure and destination
4. Use check-in during the trip

Ctrlavana works offline, so your trips and passenger lists remain accessible even without an internet connection.

<br>

## How to Create a Caravan

1. Open the **Ctrlavana** app. The home screen shows the list of caravans.  
   If you already have caravans created, tap the **"+"** button at the top of the screen.  
   If this is your first caravan, you can use either the **"+"** button at the top or the **"Add Caravan"** button in the center of the screen.

2. Fill in the caravan details.  
   The required fields are:
   - **Name**
   - **Start date**
   - **End date**
   - **Departure**
   - **Destination**

3. Tap **Save** to create the caravan.  
   After saving, the caravan will appear in the main caravan list.

Note:  
Passengers are added inside the **Caravan Details** screen after the caravan is created.

<br>

## Adding passengers

To add passengers to a caravan, you must first create the passenger in the app.

### Creating a passenger

1. Open the **Passengers** tab in the **Tab Bar**.
2. If you already have passengers created, tap the **"+"** button at the top of the screen.  
   If this is your first passenger, you can use either the **"+"** button or the **"Add Passenger"** button in the center of the screen.
3. Choose how you want to add the passenger:
   - Create a passenger manually
   - Import a contact from your contacts list
4. Fill in the required information:
   - **Name**
   - **Email**
   - **Phone number**
   - **Identification document**

After saving, the passenger will be available in the app.

### Adding the passenger to a caravan

1. Open the desired **Caravan**.
2. Go to the **Passengers** section.
3. Tap the **"+"** button.
4. Select the passenger you want to add.
5. Open the **passenger details**.
6. Tap the **bus icon with a "+"**.

The passenger will be added to the caravan.

<br>

## Check-in

- Open the caravan passenger list when boarding starts
- Mark each passenger as checked in
- Use the list to confirm who is present and who is missing

<br>

## Raffle

- Open the caravan and access the raffle tool
- Run a random draw among passengers in the app
- Save or share the result as needed for your group process

<br>

## Offline & Sync

- Ctrlavana works offline for caravan and passenger management
- Your data remains available locally without internet
- If iCloud sync is enabled, data syncs when connection returns
- On multiple devices, use the same Apple ID and keep iCloud active

<br>

## Ctrlavana Pro

Ctrlavana offers an optional Pro subscription.

Pro features include:
- Unlimited active caravans
- Export passenger lists to PDF

A 7-day free trial may be available, depending on the current App Store offer.

Subscriptions can be managed or cancelled in your Apple ID settings.

<br>

## Subscriptions & Billing (FAQ)

### Pro is active but features are still locked
- Open the Manage Subscription screen
- Tap "Restore Purchases"
- Close and reopen the app if needed

### How do I manage or cancel my subscription?
- Open your Apple ID subscription settings
- Select Ctrlavana
- Change, cancel, or review your plan status there

### I changed devices. Do I need to pay again?
- Use the same Apple ID used for the original purchase
- Restore purchases inside Ctrlavana if Pro features do not unlock automatically

<br>

## Troubleshooting

### Missing caravans or passengers

- Confirm you are viewing the correct caravan
- If using multiple devices, ensure iCloud is enabled

### Sync issues

- Ctrlavana works offline
- Data remains available locally without internet
- Allow a few moments for iCloud sync when connection returns
- Keep iCloud enabled for Ctrlavana in iOS settings

### Passenger import not visible

- Passenger import (CSV) is not available in production yet
- This feature is currently in internal/debug testing
