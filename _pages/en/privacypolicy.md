# Privacy Policy

## 1.0 Information We Collect
**Ctrlavana** collects information that is essential for the app to function properly, ensuring the best user experience.

### 1.1 Third-Party Information
- Information provided by external tools to improve our services.
- Integration with communication APIs such as WhatsApp and email to make it easier to contact your passengers.
- Use of analytics tools to monitor app performance.

### 1.2 Information You Provide
The data you provide to create caravans, locations, and passengers is collected so **Ctrlavana** can organize your caravans efficiently, but it is not sent to any service or server. This data is stored locally on your device, so you always remain in control.

<br>

## 2.0 What Personal Information We Collect and How We Collect It
The data we collect includes:

- Passenger personal information (name, email, phone) that you add in the app.
- Trip information, including origin, destination, and personal notes related to your caravans.

This data is used exclusively to help you manage your excursions and ensure you have all the information you need in an organized way. It is not sent to any service or server.

### 2.1 Third Parties We Collect Information From
Some information is collected by third parties, such as **Google Crashlytics**, which helps us monitor crashes and improve the user experience.

| Third Party | Purpose |
| :--- | :--- |
| Google Crashlytics | Crash monitoring and app performance improvements. |

<br>

## 3.0 Your Rights as a Data Subject
As a data subject, you have the right to:

1. Access the information that has been collected about you.
2. Request the deletion or modification of any information stored in the app.
3. Ensure your personal information is not shared with third parties without your consent.

**Ctrlavana** is committed to respecting all data subject rights as established by applicable law.

<br>

## 4.0 How We Store and Protect Collected Information
Information collected by **Ctrlavana** is stored locally on the user's device. This means your data stays under your control and is never sent to external servers, except for crash information, which is sent to **Google Crashlytics** to improve performance.

We use best security practices to ensure your data is protected against unauthorized access.

<br>

## 5.0 Processing and Transfers for Individuals in the EEA
If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), we ensure your personal information is processed in accordance with applicable data protection laws. We do not transfer your information outside the EEA without ensuring appropriate safeguards are in place.
